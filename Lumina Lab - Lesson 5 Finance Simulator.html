The Two Master Reports

Income Statement

What goes in: Revenue (Sales) and Expenses (Cost of Goods, Overheads).

Used for: Measuring performance over a period of time. It reveals if your core operations are actually turning a profit after running costs.

Balance Sheet

What goes in: Assets (Cash, Inventory, Equipment, IP), Liabilities (Loans, Creditors), and Owner's Equity.

Used for: A snapshot of financial stability. It shows what you own vs. what you owe, providing proof of value to banks and investors.

The Golden Rule of Business

Sales ≠ Profit | Profit ≠ Cash

A company can show massive sales but go bankrupt from lack of cash.
